As a bonus, here are some less well-known works that I loved!

  • Foundryside by Robert Jackson Bennett. Fantasy trilogy with one my fav magic systems and the greatest middle-distance friend to ever grace the page: Clef. LOVED the first book; the last two were fine.
  • Velocity Weapon by Megan E. O'Keefe. Hefty sci-fi trilogy with solid characters and another great middle-distance friend. Good read all around.
  • Time To Orbit: Unknown by Derin Edala. Web serial that was self-published as a duology; it contains a normal spaceship crewed by some normal people and nothing ever goes wrong. Ever.
